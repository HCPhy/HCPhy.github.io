Research

Magic & Participation Entropy at Measurement-Induced Transitions

Random Clifford Circuits • Quantum Automata • Participation Entropy

Studies participation entropy in random Clifford and quantum automaton circuits, showing slow critical dynamics near their measurement-induced transition points.

arXiv

Quantum Circuits with Ultraslow Dynamics

Hybrid Circuits • Glassy Dynamics • Classical Mapping

Maps hybrid quantum circuits with logarithmically slow entanglement growth to classical spin models with local multi-spin interactions and glassy finite-temperature dynamics.

arXiv

Coherent Error-Induced Phase Transition

Quantum Error Correction • Stabilizer Codes • Coherent Errors

Studies how syndrome measurements respond to coherent unitary errors and identifies a transition where logical information stops being efficiently recoverable.

arXiv

Plaquette Models & Measurement-Induced Criticality

Plaquette Models • Cellular Automata • Criticality

Relates randomized plaquette models to 1+1D cellular-automaton dynamics and connects their criticality to measurement-induced transitions in hybrid quantum circuits.

arXiv DOI

Entanglement Phase Transitions & Complexity via Ising Models

Cluster States • Transfer Matrices • Sampling Complexity

Links boundary-state entanglement transitions in measured cluster states to Ising partition functions with complex parameters and the difficulty of classical sampling.
